    About Me

    My Story

    Hi, I'm Ed, I’m a former tech headhunter turned full stack software developer in Manchester. After my dream of a military career was cut short, I spent the next 4 years pursuing a career in sales.
    As time went on, I enjoyed sales less and less and knew it was not my calling. I’d always had a love for language and writing, so I decided to learn a new language that could possibly open new doors.
    I looked at French, Russian and Arabic when a friend recommended learning a computer language as they would be the languages of the future. From the moment I typed my first line of code:
    < p>Hello, I am learning to code < / p> I’ve was hooked.
    It was what people call a quake moment, something that changes everything, something that causes an earthquake within and awakens the mind. As I began to teach myself code, I knew this is what I wanted to do. The dye was cast, and all my days since have been dedicated to becoming a developer and learning to code.
    Whether it be learning a new skills from scratch or forging a new career. I am someone who has always been willing to follow through their words and ideas with action. Whether it be joining the military as a teenager, travelling to another continent on my own, becoming a Samaritan, or starting my own business. I have always been willing to take the plunge, pick up the gauntlet and have the courage and determination to follow ideas and dreams until they become a reality.

    Who am I?

    Why Code? I love coding for the same reason I love cooking and writing, it doesn’t matter how good it is, its mine. I made it. I know what went into it and can be proud of it. Its the act of writing and creating. Bringing thoughts and ideas into reality.

    I love how code blends both logic and creativity to create truly amazing things. The fusion of logic and creativity spoke to me because they are often seen as opposites or opponents, like a red and blue team competing against each other. It is something I have always refused to believe. But in the world of coding ,they work in harmony and aid one other.

    As I taught myself HTML, CSS and javascript in preparation to join the Northcoders training programme, the deeper i fell into the world of coding, and the enchanting it became. The things I could build, the ideas I could bring to life. Simply, it puts a fire in my belly, hours can pass and I don’t even realise it.
